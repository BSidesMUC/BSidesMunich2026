BSides Munich 2026 is looking for talks and workshops from security professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned speaker or presenting for the first time, we want to hear from you!

Topics of Interest

We welcome submissions on any security-related topic, including but not limited to:

  • Offensive security & penetration testing
  • Incident response & digital forensics
  • Cloud & infrastructure security
  • Application security & DevSecOps
  • Threat intelligence & hunting
  • Hardware & IoT security
  • Privacy, policy & compliance
  • AI/ML security
  • Operational technology (OT) security
  • Community building & career development

Submission Types

Talks (30 minutes)

Standard conference presentations on any security topic. All talks are presented in the main conference day in English.

Workshops (2-4 hours)

Hands-on, interactive sessions where participants learn by doing. Workshops take place the day before the conference.

How to Submit

Submissions will be handled through our Pretalx instance. The link will be published here and on our social media channels when the CFP opens.

CFP Opens Soon

Review Process

All submissions are reviewed by our independent CFP Review Board. Reviews are conducted anonymously to ensure fairness. We aim to notify all submitters of the outcome within 2 weeks of the CFP closing date.

BSides Munich covers travel and accommodation for selected speakers. Reach out to us at cfp@bsidesmunich.org if you have any questions.
